# Learn-in-Public Newsletter

## Spark
> Turn the blog into a weekly note and build an audience around the building, not the polish.

## What Is It?
A short weekly email: what I shipped, what broke, one thing I learned. Not tutorials —
a builder's log. Ties directly to `open-kit` and `taskflow`; every week of work becomes a
post. Low production cost because the content is a byproduct of the work I'm already doing.

## Trigger
Noticed the blog only updates when I finish something big, which is rarely. A weekly cadence
forces smaller, honest updates and compounds an audience over time.
