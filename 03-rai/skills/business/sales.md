# Sales

Transform product documentation and features into compelling narratives
and visual assets. Three-stage process that moves from raw material
to polished sales content.

## Three Stages

### 1. Story Extraction
Mine source material for the core narrative.
- Identify the problem being solved
- Extract the transformation (before/after)
- Find proof points (metrics, testimonials, case studies)
- Map the competitive landscape

### 2. Emotional Register
Shape the narrative for the target audience.
- Match tone to buyer persona (executive, technical, end-user)
- Build urgency without manipulation
- Frame benefits over features
- Create memorable anchors (analogies, comparisons)

### 3. Visual Concept
Design supporting visual assets.
- Slide deck structure and key visuals
- Comparison charts and diagrams
- Before/after visualizations
- Data presentations that support the narrative

## Process

1. **Ingest**: Read product docs, specs, existing marketing
2. **Audience**: Define who we are selling to and what they care about
3. **Extract**: Pull the story from stage 1
4. **Shape**: Apply emotional register from stage 2
5. **Visualize**: Create asset concepts from stage 3
6. **Deliver**: Output narrative document, slide outline, visual specs

## Output Formats

- Sales narrative (1-2 page document)
- Pitch deck outline with slide descriptions
- One-pager or battlecard
- Email sequence draft
- Visual asset specifications

## Examples

- "Turn our API docs into a sales pitch for CTOs"
- "Create a battlecard comparing us to [competitor]"
- "Write a sales narrative for our data platform"
- "Design a pitch deck outline for investor meetings"
