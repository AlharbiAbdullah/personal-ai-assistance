# Voice Samples

> Writing samples that teach the `/writing` skill *your* voice. Drop real examples here.

The `/writing` skill drafts in your voice, but it can only learn that voice from examples.
Add 3–5 pieces of your own writing you're happy with:

- `blog/` — past blog posts or essays.
- `social/` — strong social posts or notes.
- Any long-form you'd want future drafts to sound like.

Until you add samples, the writing skill falls back to neutral defaults plus the shared
anti-AI voice rules in `03-rai/skills/writing/references/voice.md`. The more (and better)
the samples, the closer drafts land to how you actually sound.

> Tip: pick pieces that sound like *you at your best*, not just your most recent.
