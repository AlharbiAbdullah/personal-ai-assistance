# Admin

> Documents, accounts, renewals, and household maintenance. Private.

Examples of what lives here:
- `documents.md` — where important documents are (not the documents themselves, and never secrets).
- `maintenance.md` — recurring upkeep: car service, filters, warranties, renewals.
- Renewal dates worth a reminder (licenses, insurance, subscriptions).

Keep secrets out of the vault entirely — this is for *pointers and dates*, not credentials.
