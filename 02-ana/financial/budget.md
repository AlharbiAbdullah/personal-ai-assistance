## The split: bills vs subscriptions

- **`bills.md`** — fixed monthly bills paid to providers (utilities, telecom, household).
- **`subscriptions.md`** — recurring services on a card (software, media, tools).

They're tracked separately because they're paid differently and change at different rates.

## Standing rules (example posture)

1. **Debt first.** Any high-interest balance is a guaranteed loss — paying it down beats
   most investments. Clear it before deploying real money elsewhere.
2. **Pay yourself first.** A fixed savings amount moves on payday, before discretionary spend.
3. **Spend under the cap.** Operating spend has a weekly and monthly ceiling.
4. **No new high-interest debt.**
