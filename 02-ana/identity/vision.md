# Vision

> Where I'm heading, what's blocking me, what I'm actually solving.

**Last updated:** 2026-01-01

---

## Where I'm heading

To be the kind of engineer who can take an idea from "what if" to "people depend on it" mostly alone — using AI as leverage — and to do it without trading away my health or my family.

Five years out: a small portfolio of products I built, a body of writing people trust, financial breathing room, and kids who saw their dad build things he cared about.

---

## What I'm solving for

Not money for its own sake, and not a title. I'm solving for **agency** — the ability to choose what I work on, who with, and when. Skills, savings, and a reputation for shipping are the three things that buy it.

---

## What's blocking me

1. **Focus leakage.** Too many open loops; not enough finished ones.
2. **Shallow AI knowledge.** I can use the tools; I can't yet reason from first principles about them.
3. **Time math.** A full-time job, two kids, and side projects don't fit in a week without ruthless prioritization.

---

## The bet

That a disciplined person with good tools and an AI partner can out-build a team from five years ago — if they go deep on a few things instead of dabbling in many.
