# Environment

> My physical and digital work environment. Helps the assistant give setup-aware help.

**Last updated:** 2026-01-01

---

## Machines

- **Primary:** a Mac laptop (development, writing, daily driver).
- **Secondary:** a Linux box for the home lab and always-on services.
- The two are kept loosely in sync; the vault lives on both (see `03-rai/SYNC-ARCHITECTURE.md`).

---

## Desk

- A clean desk and a single large monitor. One thing at a time.
- Mechanical keyboard, decent chair, water within reach.
- Phone in another room during deep work.

---

## Digital setup

- **Editor:** a modern code editor + the terminal for most real work.
- **Terminal:** a fast terminal emulator, shell with sensible defaults, fuzzy-finder everywhere.
- **Window management:** keyboard-driven; I avoid the mouse when I can.
- **Notes / vault:** this system (`~/helm`), edited in a Markdown editor and via Claude Code.

---

## Rhythms

- Best hours are early morning and after the kids are asleep.
- Weeknights: one deep block, then off.
- I protect mornings for the hardest thing on the list.

---

## Why this file exists

So setup help, automation, and diagnostics assume *my* environment, not a generic one. When something here changes, I update it.
